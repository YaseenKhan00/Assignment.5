Ingredients:

  • - 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • - 2 tablespoons sugar
  • - 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • - 1 cup milk
  • - 1 egg
  • - 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)


Instructions:

    In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Don't overmix; it's okay if there are some lumps. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges look set, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.



Ingredients:


  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Salt & black pepper

Instructions:


  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta with garlic until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs and mix with grated cheeses.
  4. Drain spaghetti and mix with pancetta and garlic.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly mix in egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, serve immediately.



Ingredients:


  • cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract


Instructions:


  1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot griddle, cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
